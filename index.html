Why Preuro Exists: A Better Way

The government monopoly on money is a flawed experiment. A single currency, controlled by a central authority, promises unity but often delivers instability, inflation, and a loss of identity. It forces diverse nations into a one-size-fits-all model that serves a few, not the many. The great economist F.A. Hayek argued that the only path to stable and honest money is competition.

Preuro was built on this very principle. We are not creating another monopoly. We are building an ecosystem of competing currencies, restoring the historic Mark, Lira, Escudo, and others to the digital age. Our 'mother coin', $PREU, serves as the stable, transparent foundation. Its supply is fixed and cannot be inflated by political interests. This is the denationalisation of money in practice. It is the restoration of choice, and the return of economic power to the hands of the people.

The Intellectual Foundation

  • Competition is the Solution: Instead of a single currency imposed by the state, Preuro creates a market where historic currencies are reborn. The best and most trusted will thrive.
  • Stability as the Goal: With a fixed total supply, $PREU is designed to be a stable store of value, free from the political incentive to create inflation that plagues government-controlled money.
  • An End to Political Manipulation: By building on a decentralized blockchain, Preuro separates money from the state, aiming for a more honest and predictable economic environment for everyone.

How to Buy $PREU (Beginner's Guide)

Welcome to Preuro! If you are new to the world of Solana, this simple guide will help you take the first steps to join our community.

  1. Step 1: Buy SOL

    $PREU is on the Solana network, so you first need its main currency, SOL. You can securely buy SOL on a legal, regulated exchange like Coinbase, Binance, or Kraken.

  2. Step 2: Create Your Wallet

    Next, you need a digital wallet to store your coins. We recommend Phantom, a secure and easy-to-use browser extension. Install Phantom and, most importantly, save your secret phrase in a secure, offline location.

  3. Step 3: Send SOL to Your Wallet

    Copy your new Phantom wallet address. In your exchange (Coinbase, etc.), go to 'Withdraw', paste your Phantom address, and send the SOL you bought to your wallet.

  4. Step 4: Swap SOL for $PREU

    With SOL in your Phantom wallet, go to a decentralized exchange (DEX) like Raydium or Jupiter. Connect your wallet, find the SOL/$PREU pair using our official contract address (to be published here), and make the swap.
